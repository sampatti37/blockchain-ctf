What is this?

A CTF to better unnderstand the blockchainn

The purpose of this CTF is to provide users with a better understanding of blockchain technology by allowing them to experiment with different puzzles relating to blockchain technology. In total there are 7 levels, each relating to a different topic:

1. EVM Op Codes (click on the Defi App icon to go to the home page and learn how to complete this level)
2. Bash Setup
3. Cryptography
4. PTP Network
5. Smart Contract Basics
6. Exploiting a Smart Contract
7. Mint an NFT
